History 67Avenue

The history of our organization began in 2019, united by a passion for gaming and the drive to win. Over time, we realized we could do more, so we started organizing our own events and gaming tournaments. We have successfully hosted dozens of mobile gaming tournaments. Over the years, thousands of players and tens of thousands in prize money have passed through our competitions. Now, it’s time to take things to the next level and embrace new opportunities.
